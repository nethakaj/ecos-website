Company Profile

Founded in 2020. Built for Federal and Commercial Delivery.

Environmental Consulting Services, LLC (ECoS) is a Woman-Owned Small Business founded in 2020 to address a critical gap in the contracting market: the need for a single, accountable partner who can deliver both the technical infrastructure and the rigorous environmental compliance that modern projects demand.

From our headquarters in Potomac, Maryland, our team services federal agencies, state and local governments, commercial developers, and corporate facility managers across the Maryland, Virginia, and Washington D.C. region.

Every engagement is staffed by Professional Engineers, Project Managers, Certified Safety Professionals, and trained tradespeople — supported by quality control managers and field professionals operating to OSHA, EPA, and Department of Defense compliance standards.
